# Templates Library

In the **Eledo Templates Library**, you can create new templates from scratch using the Eledo Template Editor, manage templates you’ve already created, or start with ready-made Public Templates.

---

## Create a New Document Template

To create a new template:

1. Go to **Templates > My**  
2. Click the **"Create new document template"** button in the **My** section  

This will open the Eledo Template Editor.

---

## Edit an Existing Template

To edit one of your templates:

1. Go to **Templates > My**  
2. Hover over the template you want to edit  
3. Click **"Edit"**

---

## Delete a Template

To delete one of your templates:

1. Go to **Templates > My**  
2. Hover over the template you want to delete  
3. Click **"Delete"**

---

## Public Templates

In **Templates > Public**, you’ll find a collection of predefined templates that you can use as a starting point and adapt to your needs.

Available examples include:

- Invoices  
- Board items  
- Certificates  
- Letters  
- Shipping labels  
- And many more  

To use a public template:

1. Hover over the template you want to start with  
2. Click **"Create"**

You cannot accidentally modify or delete Public templates in the **Public** section — so feel free to explore and experiment.
