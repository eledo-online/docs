# Get started in two steps
Setup your automated PDF creation in two steps:

 - Prepare document template
 - Setup Integration Recipe

## Template
Once your Eledo app is installed from monday.com App Marketplace, add Eledo editor as a board view to prepare your document template.

![image](/assets/guides/MondayAddEledoView.gif)

## Integration Recipe
When your template is ready, it's time to use it in your automation. Setup Integration Recipe or Automation for your monday.com board using one of our predefined Eledo recipes or actions. Choose when your document should be created, which template should be used and where it should be uploaded.

![image](/assets/guides/MondayAddIntegrationRecipe.gif)
