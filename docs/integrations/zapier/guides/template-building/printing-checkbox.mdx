# Printing Checkbox

This guide shows how to print checkbox values into your PDF using Zapier.

---

## Step 1 — Prepare your template

In the Eledo editor:

1. Add a **Dynamic Checkbox** component  
2. In **Data (expression)**, enter a field name (for example `Fragile`)  
3. Confirm and save the template  

![Checkbox configuration](/img/integrations/make/PrintingCheckboxMake1.gif)

:::tip

Learn more about Dynamic Checkbox:  
→ [/docs/product/template-editor/components/dynamic-checkbox.mdx](/docs/product/template-editor/components/dynamic-checkbox.mdx)

:::

---

## Step 2 — Use the template in Zapier

In your Zap:

1. Add the **Eledo PDF** action  
2. Select your template  
3. Load the available fields  

Your field (for example `Fragile`) will appear.

---

## Step 3 — Map your data

1. Click into the field (for example `Fragile`)  
2. Provide a boolean value from your trigger or previous step  
   - `true` → checked  
   - `false` → unchecked  
3. Confirm the mapping  

![Mapping checkbox value](/img/integrations/zapier/ZapierPrintingCheckbox.gif)

---

## Result

When the Zap runs:

- the boolean value is passed from your trigger  
- Eledo evaluates the value  
- the checkbox is rendered as checked or unchecked
