# Update Payment Method

You can update your payment method in the **Profile → Subscription** section.

---

## How to update payment details

1. Open your **Profile**  
2. Navigate to the **Subscription** tab  
3. Click **Update Payment Details**  
4. You will be redirected to the payment gateway  
5. Update your payment method and confirm  

![Update payment details](/img/product/plans-and-billing/Payments_UpdatePaymentDetails.gif)

---

## Important notes

- The update is handled by a secure payment provider  
- Eledo does not store your payment details
