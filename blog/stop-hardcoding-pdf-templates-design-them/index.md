# Stop hardcoding your PDF templates, design them!

Most of the time exact presentation of the content is important and that's why **PDF** was developed and is widely used. But if you need to generate one, you may feel like you are living in the stone age.

<!-- truncate -->

There are many libraries and tools to render or convert documents, but sometimes even developers run into trouble when using them.

That's why **Eledo** was born. It provides a fast and easy way to prepare PDF templates with dynamic elements for developers and non-technical users alike. Let's explore its advantages.

---

## Template is all you need

Thanks to **generative design patterns**, all you need to prepare is the **template**. Everything else happens automatically.

You can immediately generate your PDF either by:

- filling in a **web form**
- using the **REST API**

The rich-text **in-browser editor** is portable and available online.

Besides paragraphs, lists, tables, or images, it includes custom components that help define dynamic parts of the template.

Some examples include:

- text fields
- conditional blocks
- repetitive blocks

These allow you to design more advanced document scenarios.

Other useful components include:

- barcodes
- QR codes
- digital signature placeholders

For those who want even more power, there are **expressions**.

Expressions allow you to:

- split dynamic data
- combine values
- perform mathematical operations
- aggregate vector data
- use boolean logic in conditional blocks

## No more wasted time (and money)

Designing templates from scratch can be time-consuming, so we are building a **public library of common templates**.

You can simply:

1. copy a template  
2. modify it  
3. adapt it to your needs  

Our mission is to **save your time while giving you maximum flexibility**.
